Welcome to Bike King Borders

Discover our range of bikes, accessories, and services. Your adventure awaits!

At Bike King Borders, we're more than just a bike shop; we're a one-stop destination for all cycling enthusiasts. Whether you're a beginner taking your first ride, a weekend warrior seeking new trails, or a seasoned pro looking for high-performance gear, we've got something for everyone.

Sales

Dive into our extensive collection of bikes ranging from sturdy mountain bikes designed to tackle rugged terrains, to sleek road bikes for those long, scenic rides. For the young and adventurous, our selection of kids' bikes promises both safety and fun. Plus, explore our assortment of accessories to enhance your cycling experience. Helmets, gloves, lights, or high-tech cycling computers - you name it, we have it.

Bike Rental

Not ready to commit to a purchase? Our rental service offers a flexible solution. Choose from a wide range of bikes and hit the trails or the road for a day, a weekend, or even longer. It's a great way to test out different styles before making a decision.

Servicing

Keep your ride smooth with our professional bike servicing. From regular maintenance to complex repairs, our skilled technicians ensure your bike is in top condition. We handle everything - gear adjustments, brake tuning, wheel alignments, and more. Plus, we offer quick, efficient service to get you back on the road in no time.

Expert Advice

Our passionate and knowledgeable team is always on hand to offer expert advice. We can help you find the perfect bike, suggest the best trails, or guide you on how to improve your riding skills.

At Bike King Borders, cycling is not just a sport or a hobby; it's a way of life. Join us and be part of a community that shares your passion. Your next cycling adventure starts here!
