Beginner Trails

Discover trails suitable for beginners.

Badaguish

Badaguish Forest is located on the flanks of a mountain ridge, near Glenmore and Aviemore. In the forest you'll find a mixture of easy gravel roads and rowdy singletrack.

Old Logging Way

Old Logging Way is a 5-mile doubletrack gravel greenway trail (with a bit of pavement on the Aviemore end) that parallels the highway, allowing the rider to stay off the busy road and connect to quality singletrack up high!
